## Scoring Guide
- 30-35 correct: Excellent! Ready for the exam!
- 25-29 correct: Good! Review a few concepts.
- 20-24 correct: Keep practicing - you're getting there!
- Below 20: More study time needed - use the visual guide!

## Tips for Improvement:
- **Binary place values:** 1, 2, 4, 8, 16, 32, 64, 128 - say them out loud!
- **Hex letters:** A=10, B=11, C=12, D=13, E=14, F=15 - make flashcards!
- **4-bit groups:** Master the 4-bit to hex table - it's your best friend!
- **Practice daily:** Do 5-10 conversions every day until automatic!
